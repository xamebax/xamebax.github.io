Airport Water Map v0.1.0

This is an early version of a map that is supposed to serve as a compilation of data on clean water accessibility at airports. The goal is to provide information on the following:

  • is there a water fountain?
  • how much does bottled water cost?
  • is tapwater safe to drink and accessible?

Don't see the airport you are looking for? It means this website has no data; only airports with data on water are shown.

If you would like to contribute with data on water, have a look at this file with a list of European airports. You can then open a pull request on Github, tweet at me, or shoot me an email.
